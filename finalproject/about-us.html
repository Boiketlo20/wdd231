About Literary Escape

Our Story: For the Bookworms Who Never Fit In

I grew up as the "odd one out." While my family loved other things, I craved stories—the kind that made galaxies bloom in my mind and characters feel like secret friends. But books were scarce, and so were people who understood them. I spent years feeling like a puzzle piece in the wrong box, whispering about my favorite reads to empty air. Now? I buy myself every book my younger self dreamed of. I geek out with friends over plot twists and underrated authors. But I’ll never forget that loneliness—the ache of loving something so much and having nowhere to share it.

That’s why Literary Escape exists.

This isn’t just a website. It’s the space I needed when I was 12, 25, or any age when books were my lifeline: A place to track your favorites like trophies. A compass for finding your next "I couldn’t put this down" read. A community where someone always gets why you’re still mad about that ending. You’re not just a reader here. You’re a voice we want to hear. A passion we celebrate. A story waiting to connect.

Welcome home, bookworm.
