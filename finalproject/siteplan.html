Literary Escape - Website Plan

Site Plan

Literary Escape

This name emphasizes how books transport readers, conveying that this website can help people find their next perfect read

Site Puprpose

The purpose is to provide a platform for anyone to discover, discuss, and share books. It connects book readers, offers books recommendations, and showcases trending books.

Scenarios

  • How do I find specific book genres? Is there a way to narrow down what I want?
  • Does this website track what I am currently reading or books I have read in the past?
  • Is there a forum or social media group for fans of a specific book or author?

Color Schema:

Primary Color: #294C60- used for headers and buttons.

Secondary Color #ADB6C4 - used for text and borders.

Accent Color 1 #FFC49B - call-to-action buttons, icons, or important text highlights.

Accent Color 2 #FFEFD3 - over effects, dividers, or subtle background details.

Typography

Large Headings: Satisfy

Sub-headings: Poppins

Body Text: Roboto Condensed
